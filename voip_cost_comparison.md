| Provider | Cost/Minute (US) | Setup Difficulty | Platform |
|----------|------------------|------------------|----------|
| Google Voice | $0.00 (FREE) | Medium-Hard | Browser/App |
| LocalPhone | ~$0.004 | Very Easy | Browser |
| VoIP.ms (Value) | ~$0.005 | Medium | Softphone/Browser |

## TOP PICK: LocalPhone (~$0.004/min)

### Why LocalPhone Wins
- Works entirely from browser (localphone.com) - no software install
- Calls to US landlines AND mobiles: $0.004/min (0.4 cents)
- $5 top-up = ~1,250 minutes (20+ hours) of calls to US
- No monthly fees, no subscriptions, no VPN needed
- Pay via credit card or PayPal
- Works from any country

### Setup Steps for LocalPhone
1. Go to www.localphone.com
2. Create a free account (email + password)
3. Click "Add Credit" and add $5 minimum
4. Click "Web Phone" in the dashboard
5. Dial US number: 1 + area code + number
6. Talk through computer microphone/speakers

## SECOND PICK: Google Voice ($0.00/min - FREE)

### The Catch
- Only officially available to US Google accounts
- Callers outside US need:
  1. VPN set to USA during account creation
  2. A US phone number for one-time verification
  3. After setup, VPN NOT needed for calls
- Once set up: unlimited free calls to all US numbers forever

### Setup Steps (if caller can manage VPN + US verification)
1. Use VPN set to United States
2. Go to voice.google.com
3. Sign in with Google account (create new US Google account if needed)
4. Verify with a US phone number (one-time)
5. Choose a free Google Voice number
6. Done - make free calls from voice.google.com in any browser

## THIRD PICK: VoIP.ms (~$0.005-0.01/min)

### Setup Steps
1. Go to voip.ms and create account
2. Add $10 minimum funds
3. Use browser-based dialer at customer portal
   OR install free softphone (Zoiper/MicroSIP) with SIP credentials
4. Dial US numbers

## NOT RECOMMENDED
- RingCentral: $20-30/month minimum, overkill for casual calls
- Skype Pay-as-you-go: $0.11/min to US, terrible value

## Notes
- All options work with just a laptop + internet + microphone
- All can call US landlines and mobile numbers
- Caller pays their own costs - no reimbursement needed
